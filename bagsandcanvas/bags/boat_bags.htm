Boat Bags

  All of our canvas is specially made for us. The yarn we use is spun to rigid specifications to create uniformity. All of our "colored cotton canvas" dyes are formulated to enable over 80% dye penetration. The yarn is then cleaned and carded before being woven into canvas. The water-repellant cotton canvas used in most of our bags is designed to last a lifetime. Whether you are traveling from your home to the office, nationally or internationally, our bags are heavy and strong enough to withstand the daily riggers of airplane storage compartments and commuter trains.

In addition to our superior canvas and leather, we use the finest quality YKK zippers, die-cast hardware, webbing and piping. This will make your decision to purchase the finest corporate accessories from Satchels by Josephson the right one.
