About Josephson


   Satchels by Josephson has been proudly producing sewn products in the USA since 1922. From a single prototype, to a small (50 piece) or large quantity run, they have earned a reputation as the leader in the industry.

    As Josephson celebrates its 77th year in business, they remain committed to producing products of exceptional quality and value. All of their bags are constructed from the highest quality materials and are designed to last a lifetime. By combining the finest materials, master craftsmanship, and numerous options, Josephson meets every customer's needs with thier stocked or custom designed bags.
